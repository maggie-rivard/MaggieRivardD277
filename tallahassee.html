Tallahassee Florida

The capitol of Florida

Tallahassee became a city in 1824.

The population of Tallahassee Florida is 196,169 people, according to the 2020 United States Census.

The 2022 America Community Survery 1-Year Estimates shows that the average income in Tallahassee Florida is $53,867. This is $15,436 lower than the average income for the state of Florida, the average income for the state of Florida is $69,303.

Tallahassee is located in the northern panhandle region of Florida.

The city of Tallahassee is urban.
