Tampa Florida

Tampa became a city in 1887.

The population of Tampa Florida is 384,959 people, according to the 2020 United States Census.

The 2022 America Community Survery 1-Year Estimates shows that the average income in Tampa Florida is $71,089. This is $1,786 higher than the average income for the state of Florida, the average income for the state of Florida is $69,303.

Tampa is located in the west-central region of Florida.

The city of Tampa is urban.
