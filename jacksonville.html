Jacksonville Florida

Jacksonville became a city in 1822 according to Britannica.

The population of Jacksonville Florida is 949,611 people according to the 2020 United States Census.

The 2022 America Community Survery 1-Year Estimates shows that the average income in Jacksonville Florida is $69,309. This is $6 higher than the average income for the state of Florida, the average income for the state of Florida is $69,303 according to the 2022 American Community Survery 1-Year Estimates.

Jacksonville is located in the north-eastern region of Florida according to Britannica.

The city of Jacksonville is urban according to Census Reporter.
